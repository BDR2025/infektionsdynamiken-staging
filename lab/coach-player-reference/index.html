Welcome to the learning journey. In studying the spread of infectious disease, the complexity of reality must be reduced. We rely on mathematical models, each highlighting particular drivers of transmission, allowing questions to be addressed clearly and results compared across perspectives. Each card links directly to the Intro, Explore, Challenge and Library sections of a minilab. Entry is flexible; however, the recommended path is Intro, then Explore and afterwards Challenge, with the Challenge inviting you to influence an epidemic wave.
