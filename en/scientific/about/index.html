Why this project exists

Models should become understandable, not intimidating.

Understanding Infection Dynamics Understanding Infection Dynamics translates epidemiological models into a learning environment that stays accessible without specialist software. Instead of merely naming abstract equations, the platform makes visible how thresholds, parameters, and interventions shape the course of a wave.

The aim is not persuasion but clarity. Learners should be able to see how a model works, which assumptions it makes, and where simple pictures stop being enough.

At its core

  • a learning journey as a calm point of entry
  • six MiniLabs as model-based workspaces
  • four recurring modes: Intro, Explore, Challenge, Library
  • coach voices as optional didactic support

How the system is structured

One interface, several layers of learning

The platform is not trying to do everything at once. It guides first, then opens space for exploration, while keeping scientific framing permanently available.

Learning journey

The hub presents the six models at a glance, marks accessible entry points, and helps learners choose the next sensible step.

MiniLabs

Each lab focuses on one model and one leading question, so differences are not only named but experienced in direct comparison.

Four modes

Intro frames, Explore opens, Challenge pushes further, and Library makes terms, assumptions, and limits explicit.

Didactic principles

Fewer barriers, more visibility

Guided to independent

The entry gives orientation first. Independent exploration gradually takes over from there.

Visual first

Curves, states, and feedback carry the explanation. Technical terminology comes in only when it genuinely helps.

Transparency over black box

Models are not mystified. Assumptions, simplifications, and limits stay visible and discussable.

Low entry threshold

The environment stays openly accessible, without log-in friction or unnecessary barriers in the first contact.

Research and openness

Openly built and technically transparent

Open Educational Resources

The platform is conceived as an open educational resource. Content should remain traceable, reusable, and not artificially gated.

  • CC BY 4.0 as the licensing baseline
  • no forced registration in the first contact
  • orientation before platform friction

Model transparency

The interface should not only show outcomes but also make the logic behind them visible. Assumptions, model limits, and simplifications therefore remain part of the presentation.
