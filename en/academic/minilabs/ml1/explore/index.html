Read the one intervention logic of ML1

ML1 has one narrow task: read how mitigation lowers effective reproduction before the visible wave changes.

R0 stays the fixed reference. Mitigation stands for public or collective measures that reduce effective contacts and therefore lower transmission pressure.

Read ML1 as one chain: fixed reference -> contact brake -> lower effective pressure -> changed end state.

Read the two open controls in the right order

Mitigation stays the main idea. Duration D only sharpens the analysis of how that pressure changes.

This fixed group size says how many people our mini example contains while you read the outbreak dynamics.

This fixed reference says how many others one infectious person would infect without a contact brake.

This says how long an infected person remains infectious before moving into recovery.

This fixed start tells you how many people begin in the infectious state before the wave unfolds.

This is the one open brake: it reduces effective contacts and therefore lowers the immediate reproduction pressure.

This fixed horizon tells you how far the simulation is allowed to run before the end state is read.

In UID this is read analytically as beta = R0 * gamma * (1 - mitigation) with gamma = 1 / D. Mitigation presses on beta; duration changes gamma.

Read the reproduction chain, not just the curve

These four cards are the actual reading task of ML1.

R0 is the reference basis. R_eff at the start shows the immediate effect of mitigation. Susceptible at the end and recovered at the end show what that pressure produced across the full horizon.

R0

R0 · Basic reproduction

Reference value

R0 is the undamped basis. It tells you how much spread pressure would exist before the contact brake lowers the effective reproduction pressure.

R_eff

R_eff · At start

Immediate effect of mitigation

This is the crucial first reading step: how much reproduction pressure is left right after mitigation is applied, while susceptibility is still high.

S

S · Susceptible at end

Who stays susceptible

At the chosen horizon, this card tells you how many people in the group of 1000 are still untouched by the outbreak dynamics.

R

R · Recovered at end

Who moved through the outbreak

At the same horizon, this card shows how many people in the group of 1000 have accumulated in recovery and therefore how much outbreak burden actually passed through the system.

Good reading means: do not only say that the wave changed. Read the chain: baseline pressure -> immediate reduction -> end state of who stayed susceptible and who moved into recovery.

The core sentence of ML1

Take-home message

In UID, mitigation presses on beta while R0 stays the reference and duration changes gamma. That is why mitigation and duration do not mean the same thing, even if both reshape the visible wave.
