Analytical guidance before the six MiniLabs Archer introduces the learning journey Archer frames the English academic journey as a model-based comparison space and keeps the emphasis on assumptions, scope and interpretive limits.

Welcome to the learning journey. I am Archer. In order to study the spread of infectious disease, the complexity of reality must be reduced through mathematical models. Each model isolates selected drivers of transmission and makes it possible to examine specific questions with greater clarity. The six cards on this page take you directly into the MiniLabs. You are free to begin wherever you wish. The recommended route, however, is to start each MiniLab with the Intro and then work through the tasks step by step to the Quiz. Should questions arise, you can reach me at any time via my portrait in the top right-hand corner. There, I can also respond to individual questions with AI support. Until then, I wish you an enjoyable and rewarding journey of discovery.
