Hey, take a look at that. You have just finished your first MiniLab, worked out how an outbreak wave builds up, and made it through the quiz.

With the Contact Brake, you could see that an outbreak is not something we just have to sit back and accept. The curve can be changed. Real outbreaks are a lot more complicated, of course, because many things happen at once. That is exactly why the work of public health authorities matters so much.

In the next MiniLab, we add another stage to the story. We will look at a model where people can already be infected before they become infectious. That makes things a bit more interesting, and a bit trickier too. Are you ready for the next MiniLab?
